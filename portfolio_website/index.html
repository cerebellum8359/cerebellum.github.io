The Life Of An Introvert

A Few Things About Myself

In the 21st century, I can only assume that the word "normal" has been strapped down with multiple meanings from different generations. However, the first "fact" that I am willing to mention about myself is that I am not a "normal" teenager;I don't act my age, more specifically, as a teenager of the 21st century I have not fully immersed myself into the technology age. Firstly, I lack almost all forms of social media presence.This is not an exaggeration, I literally do not have almost any form of social media.That includes the popular sites such as Facebook,Instagram and the youthly famous Snapchat.Secondly, I don't feel a surge of " need to be independent" syndrome that most teenagers experience.For a prolonged period of time, I was unable understand the rush to gets one's driver license or to experience new and exciting things.I rather sit at home and read books or watch t.v. I never wanted to experience more until now. My name is Abigail Cumberbatch,despite my former lack of interest in technology or any forms of communication, I am interested now.

Academic Life and Endeavors

My life is school, although it has recently been a major source of stress, frustration, anger and sometimes disappointment,however, school has also become my source of enjoyment This leads me to speak about my ultimate passion, which is to continuously learn something new and exciting. In my opinion, this can range from chemistry to trigonometry to anatomy and physiology and more recently, computer science. As one can most likely gather, my interest is swayed towards largely scientific subjects.I enjoy science rather it concerns the inner workings of the human body or a computer.I am willing to learn more about all aspects of my varying interest. As I look towards the near future (the near future being college), I am strongly considering studying Neuroscience.From the tender age of eleven, after viewing the movie "Gifted Hands" I became immediately intrigued into the world of the brain.With a career in Neuroscience much like a career in Computer science new information is being founded everyday I will have the opportunity to be part of the discovery.
